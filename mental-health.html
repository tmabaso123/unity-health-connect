Free Mental Health Screening

Take our mental health screening to help identify and address any issues early. Answer the following questions:

Questions

1. Over the last two weeks, how often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?




2. Over the last two weeks, how often have you experienced little interest or pleasure in doing things?




3. Over the last two weeks, how often have you had trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much?




4. Over the last two weeks, how often have you felt tired or had little energy?




5. Over the last two weeks, how often have you had a poor appetite or overeating?




6. Over the last two weeks, how often have you felt bad about yourself – or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down?




7. Over the last two weeks, how often have you had trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television?




8. Over the last two weeks, how often have you felt restless or fidgety?




9. Over the last two weeks, how often have you felt that you are moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed? Or the opposite – being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual?




10. Over the last two weeks, how often have you thought that you would be better off dead, or of hurting yourself in some way?
